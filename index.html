Mastermind game

How to play ?

To win, you have to guess the color list chosen by the game. This list is secret, it will help you we'll have to look!

For this, you have a number of lines available to try to find the combination. You have to fill in the holes in the line with the colours at the bottom of the game.
Once filled, you just have to validate, the game will give you clues to know if a color is present but not well-placed, or if you have it well-placed.

NOTE: The colors of the clues are not arranged in the same way as the colors in the game, so the clues do not indicate exactly which colors are well placed or not!
